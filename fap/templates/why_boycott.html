Why Boycott

It’s a Form of Non-Violent Resistance: Boycotts are a form of non-violent resistance that can be an effective way to pressure Israel to change its policies and respect the rights of Palestinians.

It’s a Moral Imperative: Boycotts can be a moral imperative for those who believe in human rights and justice. By boycotting Israel, individuals can make a statement about their values and ethics.

It’s a Form of Solidarity: Boycotts can be a way to show solidarity with the Palestinian people and their struggle for justice and human rights.

It’s a Way to Promote Change: Boycotts can be an effective way to promote change and pressure Israel to end its occupation and respect the rights of Palestinians.
